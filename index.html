PeliParty Prototype

PeliParty is an interactive watch party app where you stream movies together with hosts — real influencers and film critics who react on camera in real time, just like watching with friends.

How it works
1
Pick a movie and tap Join Now to enter the watch party
2
Watch the movie while hosts react live on camera — blue outline shows who's speaking
3
Hit the reaction buttons — your emotion appears on screen with a sound effect
4
Chat with thousands of viewers in real time
Host types
Cam on — host streams live video reaction
Cam off — host joins audio-only with avatar
Blue outline — this host is currently speaking
